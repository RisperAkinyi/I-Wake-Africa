About Us

iWake Africa is registered as a Limited Company pursuant to the Companies Act, 2015 of the Laws of Kenya.iWake Africa was established in 2016 to improve mental health outcomes of the people we serve through knowledge resources, targeted self-help tools, workplace training, workshops and seminars and consultancy. The organisational offices are currently located in Nyeri County, Kenya, with projected future offices in key cities of Kenya and the East and Central African region.iWake Africa activities are carried out through targeted training for vulnerable groups, as an implementing agency for the government and non-governmental organisations, knowledge centres, print and electronic information on mental wellness, informative website and quarterly publications.
